# Glossary - Catalog Service

**Service:** Catalog Service  
**Category:** Reference  
**Last Updated:** January 11, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0.0

> **Quick Summary:** Essential terms and definitions specific to the Catalog Service.

---

## B

**Base Price**: Platform-wide reference price for a material or labor service. Vendors set their own prices relative to this.

**Bulk Operations**: Admin endpoints for mass updates (activate materials, update prices, delete items).

**Buyer**: Individual home builder or small construction firm purchasing materials and labor.

---

## C

**Category**: Hierarchical grouping for materials (Cement, Bricks) or labor (Masonry, Carpentry).

**Catalog**: Complete collection of materials and labor services available on RealServ platform.

**Certification Required**: Whether labor service requires formal certification (e.g., electrician license).

---

## G

**GST Percentage**: Goods and Services Tax rate (typically 18% for most materials, 28% for cement).

---

## H

**HSN Code**: Harmonized System of Nomenclature code for GST compliance (e.g., "2523" for cement).

**Hourly Rate**: Labor cost per hour (â‚¹/hour).

**Hyderabad**: Primary service area for RealServ (Telangana, India).

---

## I

**Inventory**: Vendor-specific stock of materials with pricing and availability.

**Is Active**: Boolean flag indicating if material/labor/category is currently available on platform.

**Is Popular**: Boolean flag marking featured/popular items for homepage display.

---

## L

**Labor Category**: Skilled labor service (e.g., "Skilled Mason", "Electrician").

**Lead Time Days**: Number of days required for vendor to fulfill an order after placement.

---

## M

**Material**: Construction material (cement, bricks, steel, sand, paints, etc.).

**Min Order Quantity**: Minimum quantity required for an order (vendor-specific).

**Min Booking Hours**: Minimum hours required to book labor service (typically 4-8 hours).

---

## P

**Price History**: Audit trail tracking all price changes with timestamps and reasons.

**Popular Material**: Featured material displayed prominently on platform homepage.

---

## S

**SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit - unique identifier for materials (e.g., "CEM-OPC53-UT").

**Skill Level**: Labor expertise level (1=Helper, 2=Skilled, 3=Expert).

**Stock Alert Threshold**: Inventory level that triggers low stock notification.

**Stock Quantity**: Current available inventory for a vendor-material combination.

---

## U

**Unit**: Measurement unit for materials (bag, kg, ton, piece, cft, sqft).

---

## V

**Vendor**: Supplier of construction materials or provider of skilled labor.

**Vendor Inventory**: Vendor-specific material stock, pricing, and availability data.

**Vendor Labor Availability**: Vendor's offered labor services with rates and worker counts.

**Vendor Price**: Vendor-specific price (may differ from base price).
